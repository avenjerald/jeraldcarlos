About Me

I’ve worked as an account manager for the past couple of years in tech agencies specializing in web design and development for brands using Shopify. My three years of experience as a team leader of a merchant support team for Shopify led me to explore helping brands grow their e-commerce websites through optimized user experience.

Throughout my 5+ years of experience with Shopify, I’ve learned Shopify theme development using HTML, CSS/ Tailwind CSS, and template languages Shopify Liquid and JSON. When I have the chance, I also help with some front-end development work within the Shopify agencies I’ve worked with.

I realized how fun and satisfying it is to bring web designs to life, not to mention resolving issues and bugs, so I decided to transition into a front-end web developer role.

I created this personal website to practice and improve my skills and to serve as my portfolio. If you’re looking for a front-end developer or need someone to collaborate with, hit me up!
